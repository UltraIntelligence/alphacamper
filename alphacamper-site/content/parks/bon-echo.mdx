## How cancellations work at Bon Echo Provincial Park

Bon Echo cancellations happen for ordinary reasons. Weather turns bad. Someone cannot get Friday off. A family decides to shorten the trip. Another group finds a different park that better matches their gear or their drive time. Those decisions release real inventory back into the system.

When that happens, the opening is live for anyone who can complete a valid booking first. There is no protected lane for people who were watching it last week. An alert tells you a site exists. It does not finish the job.

This is where a lot of families get close and still lose. They click immediately, then spend the next minute typing the same fields everybody else is typing. That minute is the whole race.

## How to actually get the site you want

1. Be signed in to Ontario Parks before booking opens or before an alert comes in.
2. Keep your party info, vehicle details, and payment method ready.
3. Treat cancellations as a main path, not just a lucky backup.
4. Make your goal speed, not perfection. You can compare details before the site opens, not after.
